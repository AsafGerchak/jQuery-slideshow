Top 5 T.O.

Czehoski

Czehoski's rooftop patio is one to fill up fast, meaning the determined patio-seekers should head straight up to its second floor (and bypass the provocative art displayed on Czehoski's walls). This patio is pretty well enclosed with a shingled wall border and wood beam frame, so a breeze can sometimes be hard to come by. Nevertheless, the mood up top is pretty laid-back and relaxed, with a wall of bench seating, a spattering of patio umbrellas, and cold beers all around.
